What we're about

SMF is a freelancing organization, we're fairly new to the competition but we're looking to make a
name for ourselves in the market of web development. They way we like to do things is fairly simple, we
work on a project to project basis until we can get more freelancers working for us. We accept any projects
you have to offer us and our pricing reflects that. Most of the time, the pricing depends on the type of
work we're going to be doing because some languages require more time to work on than others (looking at you JS).
But we always get our projects finished within a reasonable amount of time. If you are looking for someone with
experience on MySQL, we have Vulcan, who is in the process of learning it and if you're looking for JavaScript, we
have ShockEMP and Macky who are both partially experienced in the field. Currently, our strongest languages are HTML5,
CSS3, and JavaScript with some Python and Ruby mixed in. Currently, we don't have anyone learning Perl but that may soon
change if demand increases. Our goal is currently to provide our clients with the necessary elements to manage a website
while we also offer web management. The webmaster is Macky, who has the experience required to maintain your website
if the need is there. Web management can be requested through our Contact forms on the next page if that's what you're
looking for. Thank you for visiting the official SMF website and we hope you consider us for your next website.

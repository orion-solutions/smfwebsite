About Orion

Orion eSports is a multi-game competetive club that is owned by SMF.
Orion was created by players who loved gaming and the social aspects
it can bring to the table. Our players are skilled in multiple areas of different games.
The main games played by Orion are League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
and Gigantic. More games will most likely be added in the future.

Orion plays in tournaments in communities such as Faceit and CEVO for CS:GO and CL (Compete League) for League of Legends. Our players are
smart and make tactical decisions based on the situation as it unfolds before them.

Orion's Players

Currently, Orion's roster includes the following players:

  • Macky; Macky is the captain of the team and mainly focuses on League of Legends but plays CS:GO as well
  • Vadenator; Vade is the co-captain of the team and organizes the matches for the most part Vade plays only League of Legends
  • Vulcan; Vulcan is the cool-headed player who focuses on both League of Legends and CS:GO
  • GhostlyGore; Ghostly is the shotcaller in games and helps the team keep cool when having a bad game
  • Lagoon; Lagoon is the go big or go home kind of player who focuses on making big plays in game, Lagoon plays mostly CS:GO but some League as well
